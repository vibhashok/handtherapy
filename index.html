Hand Therapy Exercises

Our therapists recommend these simple exercises to improve hand strength, flexibility, and coordination. Perform them daily to enhance your recovery and hand health.

Finger Stretch

Place your hand flat on a surface and gently stretch each finger out, then relax. Repeat 10 times per hand to reduce stiffness.

Grip Strengthening

Use a soft stress ball or therapy putty. Squeeze and release slowly to build grip strength and endurance.

Thumb Flex

Move your thumb across your palm toward your little finger and hold for 5 seconds. Repeat 10 times to improve thumb flexibility.

Finger Lift

Lay your hand flat on a table and lift each finger one at a time. This helps enhance tendon glide and control.
